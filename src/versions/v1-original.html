The Common Mistakes

Where established brands underestimate Amazon.

Treating Amazon like a media buy

Most brands pour ad spend onto a broken foundation. If your listings aren't converting, more spend just accelerates the bleed. Advertising amplifies what's already there, good or bad.

Missing the brand halo effect

What happens on Amazon doesn't stay on Amazon. Poor reviews, inconsistent content, and pricing chaos erode brand perception across every channel: DTC, retail, and beyond.

Reactive instead of proactive

Most brands only act when something breaks. The real opportunity is in constant optimization: catching the signal before it becomes a problem and staying ahead of competitors who aren't watching as closely.
