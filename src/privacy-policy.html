Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Stonecutter NYC ("Stonecutter," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Website analytics — We collect anonymized data about how visitors use our website (pages visited, session duration, referral source). This data does not identify individual users.

Contact form submissions — When you reach out through our website, we collect your name, email address, company name, and the content of your message.

Client onboarding information — Clients who engage our services provide business information necessary to manage their Amazon presence, including account credentials, product data, and performance metrics.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

— Respond to inquiries and communicate with prospective clients

— Provide Amazon management services to authorized clients

— Improve our website and service offerings

— Comply with applicable legal obligations

Amazon SP-API Data

Stonecutter accesses the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) on behalf of authorized selling partner clients. Data accessed through the SP-API is used solely to provide services to the specific authorized selling partner who granted access. We do not aggregate data across client accounts, sell or share SP-API data with third parties, or use it for any purpose outside of directly serving the authorizing selling partner.

Brand Analytics data accessed through the SP-API is used exclusively to identify keyword opportunities and optimize listings and advertising strategy for each individual client account. This data is never used for benchmarking, sold, or shared with any third party.

Data Storage & Security

Client data is stored securely using industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Client data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our business, subject to confidentiality agreements. We do not share SP-API data with any third parties under any circumstances.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@stonecutter.nyc. We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@stonecutter.nyc.
