Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Stonecutter NYC ("Stonecutter," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including name, email address, company name, and messages submitted through our contact form. We also collect anonymized website analytics data to understand how visitors use our site. Clients who engage our services provide business information necessary to manage their Amazon presence.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, provide Amazon management services to authorized clients, improve our website experience, and comply with applicable legal obligations. We do not use contact form information for unsolicited marketing.

Amazon SP-API Data

Stonecutter accesses the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) on behalf of authorized selling partner clients. Data accessed through the SP-API is used solely to provide services to the specific authorized selling partner who granted access. We do not aggregate data across client accounts, sell or share SP-API data with third parties, or use it for any purpose outside of directly serving the authorizing selling partner.

Brand Analytics data accessed through the SP-API is used exclusively to identify keyword opportunities and optimize listings and advertising strategy for each individual client account. This data is never used for benchmarking, sold, or shared with any third party.

Data Storage & Security

We store data using industry-standard security measures. Client data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. We regularly review our data practices to ensure appropriate protections are in place.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in delivering our services, under confidentiality agreements. We do not share SP-API data with any third parties under any circumstances.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To make a request, contact us at privacy@stonecutter.nyc.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@stonecutter.nyc.
