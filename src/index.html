The Common Mistakes

Where established brands underestimate Amazon.

Treating Amazon like a media buy

Most brands pour ad spend onto a broken foundation. If your listings aren't converting, more spend just accelerates the bleed.

Missing the brand halo effect

What happens on Amazon doesn't stay on Amazon. Poor reviews and pricing chaos erode brand perception across every channel.

Reactive instead of proactive

Most brands only act when something breaks. The real opportunity is in constant optimization and catching signals before they become problems.
